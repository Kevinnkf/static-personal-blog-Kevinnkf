Comfort City!

Bandung's natural wealth never disappoints The cool atmosphere and green views in various corners of the highlands in Bandung are very suitable as a place to relax the mind. Pesona friends can try visiting the beautiful tea gardens in the Ciwidey area and enjoy the fresh air freely. If you want a more popular destination, exploring every side of the pine forest in Cikole, which is beautiful and spectacular, is the answer, Pesona Friends! Apart from that, there is also exciting glamping with a choice of stunning natural panoramas, such as at Lakeside Rancabali, The Lodge Maribaya, and Trizara Resort. What you shouldn't miss, especially if it's not a natural tourist attraction to Mount Tangkuban Perahu. Yes! Pesona Friends must witness the beauty of the crater which is closely related to the West Javanese folklore entitled Sangkuriang. According to the story, the shape of Mount Tangkuban Perahu, which looks like an upside down mountain, came from a boat made by Sangkuriang which was a requirement for marrying the most beautiful girl who turned out to be his mother, Dayang Sumbi. However, this condition failed to be fulfilled until Sangkuriang kicked the boat until it capsized and according to the story, that was the beginning of the creation of Mount Tangkuban Perahu.
